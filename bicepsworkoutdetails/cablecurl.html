Cable Curl

Description

The cable curl offers constant tension on the biceps throughout the range of motion, helping to build size and strength with a controlled motion.

Posture Guide

- Stand facing the cable machine with a straight bar or EZ-bar attachment
- Grip the bar with an underhand grip, arms fully extended
- Curl the bar toward your chest while keeping elbows in place
- Squeeze at the top, then slowly return to the start
