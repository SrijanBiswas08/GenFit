Barbell Curl

Description

The barbell curl is a fundamental biceps exercise focused on building mass and strength by curling a weighted barbell with controlled form.

Posture Guide

- Stand upright holding a barbell with an underhand grip
- Keep your elbows close to your torso
- Curl the barbell while keeping the upper arms stationary
- Squeeze the biceps at the top, then slowly lower the weight
