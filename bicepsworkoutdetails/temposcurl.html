Tempo Curl

Description

The tempo curl manipulates the speed of the lift and lower phases to increase time under tension and enhance bicep hypertrophy.

Posture Guide

- Use dumbbells or a barbell with underhand grip
- Curl up for 1–2 seconds
- Pause at the top for 1 second
- Lower slowly for 3–4 seconds
- Keep elbows stationary and avoid swinging
