Incline Dumbbell Curl

Description

The incline dumbbell curl stretches the biceps more than standard curls, improving the range of motion and helping develop the long head of the biceps.

Posture Guide

- Sit back on an incline bench with arms hanging straight down
- Hold dumbbells in both hands with palms facing forward
- Curl both dumbbells simultaneously
- Avoid swinging and keep shoulders relaxed
