Preacher Machine Curl

Description

The preacher machine curl isolates the biceps by eliminating momentum and shoulder involvement. It enhances peak contraction and controlled eccentric movement.

Posture Guide

- Sit on the preacher bench with your arms over the pad
- Grip the handles or bar with an underhand grip
- Curl the weight towards your shoulders
- Lower it back slowly while keeping arms in contact with the pad
