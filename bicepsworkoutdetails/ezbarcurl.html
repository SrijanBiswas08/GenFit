EZ-Bar Curl

Description

The EZ-bar curl reduces wrist strain compared to the straight barbell while still effectively targeting the biceps. Itâ€™s great for both strength and hypertrophy.

Posture Guide

- Stand upright holding the EZ-bar with an underhand grip
- Hands should be on the angled grip sections
- Keep elbows fixed to your sides
- Curl the bar up, squeeze the biceps, then lower with control
