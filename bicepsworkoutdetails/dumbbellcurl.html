Dumbbell Curl

Description

The dumbbell curl allows for unilateral biceps training, improving muscle balance and isolation. Each arm lifts independently for a more controlled movement.

Posture Guide

- Stand with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing forward
- Keep elbows close to your torso
- Curl both dumbbells simultaneously or alternately
- Squeeze the biceps at the top and lower slowly
