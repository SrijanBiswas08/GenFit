21s Curl

Description

The 21s curl breaks the bicep curl into three ranges of motion for a total of 21 reps per set, increasing time under tension and maximizing pump.

Posture Guide

- Stand with a barbell or EZ-bar using an underhand grip
- Perform 7 reps from bottom to midpoint
- Perform 7 reps from midpoint to top
- Perform 7 full-range curls
- Maintain strict form and avoid swinging
