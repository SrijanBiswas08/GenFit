Leaning Lateral Raise

Description

The Leaning Lateral Raise is a shoulder isolation exercise that enhances lateral deltoid development. By leaning away from the anchor point, it increases the range of motion and tension on the delts throughout the lift.

Posture Guide

- Stand beside a sturdy object and grasp it with one hand
- Lean your body slightly away, keeping the supporting arm straight
- Hold a dumbbell in the opposite hand
- Raise the dumbbell to shoulder height while maintaining a slight elbow bend
- Control the descent and repeat for reps before switching sides
