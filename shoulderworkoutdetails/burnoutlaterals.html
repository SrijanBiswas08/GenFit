Burnout Laterals

Description

Burnout Laterals are high-rep lateral raise sets used at the end of a shoulder workout to exhaust the lateral deltoids. These are typically done with light weights or resistance bands and are aimed at maximizing muscle fatigue and stimulating hypertrophy.

Posture Guide

- Stand upright with a dumbbell in each hand or use resistance bands
- Keep a slight bend in your elbows and raise your arms to the sides
- Lift until your arms are parallel to the ground
- Focus on strict form and continuous movement
- Perform high reps (20â€“30) with minimal rest
