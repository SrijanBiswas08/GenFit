Seated Dumbbell Press

Description

The Seated Dumbbell Press is a shoulder-strengthening exercise that targets the deltoid muscles, particularly the front and middle heads. Performing it while seated minimizes lower body involvement and promotes better isolation of the shoulders.

Posture Guide

- Sit on a bench with back support and hold a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height
- Keep your feet flat on the ground and core engaged
- Press both dumbbells upward until your arms are fully extended overhead
- Avoid locking your elbows and keep your wrists straight
- Lower the dumbbells back to shoulder height with control
