Dumbbell Lateral Raise

Description

The Dumbbell Lateral Raise is a shoulder isolation exercise that primarily targets the lateral deltoids. It helps to build width and definition in the shoulders, contributing to a more balanced upper body physique.

Posture Guide

- Stand tall with a dumbbell in each hand, arms at your sides
- Keep your back straight and core engaged
- Raise both dumbbells out to the sides until they reach shoulder height
- Pause briefly at the top, then slowly lower the dumbbells
- Avoid swinging or using momentum
