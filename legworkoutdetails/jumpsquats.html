Jump Squats

Description

Jump Squats are a plyometric exercise that strengthens the legs and glutes while also improving explosive power and cardiovascular endurance.

Posture Guide

- Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
- Lower into a squat by bending the knees and pushing the hips back
- Explode upwards into a jump, extending your body fully
- Land softly and return immediately into the next squat
- Keep the chest upright and core engaged throughout
