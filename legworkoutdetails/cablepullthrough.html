Cable Pull-Through

Description

The Cable Pull-Through is a posterior chain exercise that emphasizes the glutes and hamstrings while minimizing lower back strain. Itâ€™s a great movement for developing hip hinge mechanics and building glute strength.

Posture Guide

- Stand facing away from a cable machine with a rope attachment set to the lowest point
- Grasp the rope between your legs and take a few steps forward to create tension
- Keep a soft bend in the knees and hinge at the hips while maintaining a flat back
- Drive through your heels and extend your hips forward to return to standing
- Squeeze the glutes at the top and repeat
