Romanian Deadlift

Description

The Romanian Deadlift (RDL) is a strength training exercise that targets the hamstrings, glutes, and lower back. Unlike the conventional deadlift, the RDL emphasizes the eccentric phase with a slight knee bend and a hip hinge to stretch and strengthen the posterior chain.

Posture Guide

- Stand with feet hip-width apart holding a barbell in front of thighs
- Keep knees slightly bent and spine neutral
- Hinge at the hips while keeping the barbell close to the legs
- Lower the bar until you feel a stretch in your hamstrings (mid-shin level)
- Drive hips forward to return to the standing position
