Hack Squat

Description

The Hack Squat is a compound leg exercise that primarily targets the quadriceps, glutes, and hamstrings. Performed using a hack squat machine, it provides a guided path of movement that reduces stress on the lower back while allowing you to load your legs heavily.

Posture Guide

- Position yourself on the hack squat machine with your back and shoulders against the pads
- Place your feet shoulder-width apart on the platform with toes slightly pointed out
- Lower your body by bending your knees and hips, keeping your back flat
- Descend until your thighs are at least parallel to the platform
- Push through your heels to return to the starting position
- Keep your core engaged throughout the movement
