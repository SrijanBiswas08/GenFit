Step Ups

Description

Step Ups are a unilateral lower body exercise that strengthens the quads, glutes, and hamstrings. They also improve balance, coordination, and single-leg strength, making them great for athletes and functional training.

Posture Guide

- Use a sturdy bench or box around knee height
- Stand tall, holding dumbbells at your sides (optional)
- Step up with one foot, pressing through the heel to lift your body
- Bring your other foot up to stand on the box
- Step down with the same foot you stepped up with
- Keep your torso upright and avoid leaning forward
- Alternate or complete all reps on one side before switching
