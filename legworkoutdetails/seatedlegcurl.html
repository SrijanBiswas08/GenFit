Seated Leg Curl

Description

The Seated Leg Curl is an isolation exercise that targets the hamstrings. It involves curling your lower legs back against resistance while seated, helping to build strength and muscle mass in the back of your thighs.

Posture Guide

- Sit comfortably on the leg curl machine with your back against the pad
- Position the roller pad just above your heels
- Adjust the thigh pad to hold your legs in place
- Curl your legs back as far as possible while keeping your upper body still
- Squeeze your hamstrings at the peak contraction
- Slowly return to the starting position without letting the weights drop
