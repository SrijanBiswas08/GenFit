Bulgarian Split Squat

Description

The Bulgarian Split Squat is a lower-body unilateral exercise that primarily targets the quads, hamstrings, and glutes. It also challenges balance and core stability, making it a powerful addition to leg day training routines.

Posture Guide

- Stand facing away from a bench, place one foot behind you on the bench
- Keep your front foot flat and planted forward
- Lower your hips by bending the front knee to 90 degrees
- Keep your torso upright and core engaged
- Drive through the front heel to return to the starting position
