Incline Barbell Bench Press

Description

The Incline Barbell Bench Press targets the upper chest and front deltoids. It’s performed on an incline bench using a barbell.

Posture Guide

- Set the bench to a 30–45° incline
- Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width
- Lower the bar to upper chest in a controlled motion
- Press the bar up without locking your elbows completely
