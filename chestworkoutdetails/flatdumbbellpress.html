Flat Dumbbell Press

Description

The Flat Dumbbell Press works the middle chest while also engaging stabilizer muscles. It allows a greater range of motion compared to the barbell version.

Posture Guide

- Lie flat on a bench with a dumbbell in each hand
- Keep feet flat on the ground
- Press the dumbbells upward until arms are extended
- Lower them back down slowly in a controlled motion
