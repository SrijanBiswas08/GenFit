Flat Cable Fly

Description

The Flat Cable Fly isolates the chest muscles, providing constant tension throughout the range of motion. It's effective for building the inner chest.

Posture Guide

- Set the pulleys to chest height and lie on a flat bench
- Hold the handles with arms extended and slight bend in elbows
- Bring the handles together in a wide arc above your chest
- Return slowly to the starting position
