Isometric Plate Squeeze

Description

The Isometric Plate Squeeze is a chest activation exercise that builds mind-muscle connection and focuses on the inner chest by maintaining a static contraction while pressing a weight plate between the hands.

Posture Guide

- Stand upright holding a weight plate at chest level with palms facing each other
- Squeeze the plate with both hands as hard as possible
- Keep elbows slightly bent and shoulders relaxed
- Hold the squeeze for a designated amount of time while maintaining posture
- Breathe steadily throughout the hold
