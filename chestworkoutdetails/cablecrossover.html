Cable Crossover

Description

The Cable Crossover is a popular isolation movement for the chest, focusing on the inner and lower pectorals. It provides constant tension and can be varied by adjusting pulley height.

Posture Guide

- Set the pulleys above shoulder height and grab the handles
- Step forward into a split stance and lean slightly forward
- With a slight bend in your elbows, bring your hands together in a wide arc
- Squeeze your chest at the midpoint
- Slowly return to the starting position with control
