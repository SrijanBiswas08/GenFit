Incline Dumbbell Press

Description

The Incline Dumbbell Press is a compound chest exercise that targets the upper pectoral muscles while also engaging the shoulders and triceps.

Posture Guide

- Set the bench to a 30-45 degree incline
- Lie back with a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder level
- Press the weights upward while keeping your core tight
- Lower slowly to the starting position with control
