Close-Grip Push-Up

Description

The Close-Grip Push-Up is a bodyweight exercise that primarily targets the triceps, while also engaging the chest and shoulders. Itâ€™s a great option for developing upper body strength and definition using minimal equipment.

Posture Guide

- Begin in a traditional push-up position, but place your hands closer together under your chest
- Keep your elbows tucked close to your body
- Lower your body in a controlled manner, keeping your core tight
- Push back up, fully extending your arms
