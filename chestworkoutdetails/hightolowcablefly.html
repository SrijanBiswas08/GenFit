High-to-Low Cable Fly

Description

The High-to-Low Cable Fly emphasizes the lower pectorals by guiding the arms in a downward arc, creating a deep chest contraction through constant cable resistance.

Posture Guide

- Set the pulleys to the highest position
- Stand in the center, feet staggered for balance
- Grip the handles with arms slightly bent
- Pull the handles down and forward in a wide arc
- Squeeze your chest at the bottom, then return slowly
