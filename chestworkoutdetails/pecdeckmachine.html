Pec Deck Machine

Description

The Pec Deck Machine is a chest isolation exercise that emphasizes the inner portion of the pectorals. It provides consistent tension and helps build muscle definition.

Posture Guide

- Adjust the seat height so your arms are parallel to the floor
- Place your forearms against the pads and grip the handles
- Keep your back flat and chest up
- Bring the handles together in front of your chest, squeezing your pecs
- Slowly return to the starting position
