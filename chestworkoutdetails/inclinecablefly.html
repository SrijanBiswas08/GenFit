Incline Cable Fly

Description

The Incline Cable Fly targets the upper chest using cables for constant tension throughout the movement.

Posture Guide

- Set the bench to a slight incline and position it between two cable towers
- Grab the handles and lie back on the bench
- Start with arms extended and slightly bent, palms facing each other
- Lower the cables in a wide arc until you feel a stretch in your chest
- Bring the handles back together using your chest muscles
