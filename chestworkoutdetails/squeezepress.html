Squeeze Press

Description

The Squeeze Press is a variation of the dumbbell chest press that emphasizes constant tension on the chest by keeping the dumbbells pressed together throughout the movement.

Posture Guide

- Lie on a flat bench holding two dumbbells close together at chest level
- Press the dumbbells together and squeeze your chest
- Extend your arms upward while maintaining the squeeze
- Slowly lower back to the start position without letting the dumbbells separate
- Keep core tight and feet flat on the ground
