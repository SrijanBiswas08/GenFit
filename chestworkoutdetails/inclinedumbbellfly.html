Incline Dumbbell Fly

Description

The Incline Dumbbell Fly emphasizes the upper chest using a controlled dumbbell arc motion on an incline bench.

Posture Guide

- Set the bench to a 30–45° incline
- Hold a dumbbell in each hand with palms facing each other
- Lower the weights in a wide arc to the sides until chest is stretched
- Squeeze chest and bring dumbbells back to the top
