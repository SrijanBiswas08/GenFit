Lying Triceps Extension

Description

The Lying Triceps Extension, commonly known as Skull Crushers, is an isolation movement that effectively targets the triceps, especially the long head.

Posture Guide

- Lie flat on a bench holding a barbell or EZ bar
- Extend your arms straight up over your shoulders
- Lower the bar toward your forehead or just behind your head
- Keep elbows in and avoid flaring
- Extend your arms back to the starting position
