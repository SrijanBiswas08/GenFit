Drop Set Cable Pushdowns

Description

This drop set method for cable pushdowns intensifies the triceps workout by reducing the weight in stages, extending the set to complete fatigue.

Posture Guide

- Attach a bar or rope to a high pulley
- Stand upright with elbows close to the body
- Push down until arms are fully extended
- Decrease the weight after failure and continue immediately
- Repeat 2â€“3 drops until triceps are fully burned out
