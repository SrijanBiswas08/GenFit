Bench Dips

Description

Bench dips are a bodyweight exercise that effectively targets the triceps. They can be performed using a bench or any stable surface to provide support and range of motion.

Posture Guide

- Sit on the edge of a bench and place your hands beside your hips
- Slide forward off the bench, keeping legs extended or bent
- Lower your body by bending your elbows until your arms form a 90-degree angle
- Push through your palms to lift back up
