Dumbbell Overhead Extension

Description

The Dumbbell Overhead Extension is an effective isolation movement for the triceps. This exercise emphasizes the long head of the triceps by putting it in a fully stretched position during the movement.

Posture Guide

- Sit on a bench with back support and hold one dumbbell with both hands
- Extend your arms overhead with elbows close to your ears
- Slowly lower the dumbbell behind your head by bending your elbows
- Raise the dumbbell back overhead by extending your elbows fully
