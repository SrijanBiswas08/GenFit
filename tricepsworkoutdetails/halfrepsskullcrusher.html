Half-Reps Skull Crusher

Description

This skull crusher variation limits the range of motion to the bottom half, maximizing time under tension for greater triceps fatigue and growth.

Posture Guide

- Lie flat on a bench with an EZ-bar or dumbbells
- Lower the weight just past your forehead
- Perform reps in the bottom half only (without locking out)
- Keep elbows steady and avoid flaring
- Control the movement throughout
