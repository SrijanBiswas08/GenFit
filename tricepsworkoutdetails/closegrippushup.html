Close-Grip Push-Up

Description

The close-grip push-up is a variation of the traditional push-up that emphasizes the triceps. The narrow hand placement places greater tension on the arms during the movement.

Posture Guide

- Begin in a standard push-up position
- Position your hands closer together, directly under your shoulders
- Keep your elbows tucked in as you lower your body
- Push through your palms to return to the top
