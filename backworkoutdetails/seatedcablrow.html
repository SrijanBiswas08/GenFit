Seated Cable Row

Description

The seated cable row is a back-strengthening exercise that targets the lats, rhomboids, and middle traps, enhancing posture and muscular balance.

Posture Guide

- Sit on the bench with feet on the platform
- Grab the handle with both hands
- Keep your chest up and back straight
- Pull the handle towards your waist while squeezing your shoulder blades
- Return slowly to the start position
