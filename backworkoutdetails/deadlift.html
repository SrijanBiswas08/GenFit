Deadlift

Description

The deadlift is a fundamental strength training movement that targets the posterior chain including glutes, hamstrings, lower back, and traps.

Posture Guide

- Stand with feet hip-width apart, bar over midfoot
- Grip the bar just outside the knees
- Hinge at the hips, keep back straight and chest up
- Drive through the heels to lift the bar
- Stand fully upright with hips and shoulders rising together
- Lower the bar in a controlled manner
