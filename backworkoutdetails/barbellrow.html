Barbell Row

Description

The barbell row is a compound back exercise that targets the lats, rhomboids, and rear delts, helping to build thickness and strength in the upper and mid-back.

Posture Guide

- Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
- Grip the barbell with an overhand grip
- Bend your knees slightly and hinge at the hips
- Pull the barbell towards your lower ribcage
- Squeeze the shoulder blades together
- Lower the bar slowly and repeat
