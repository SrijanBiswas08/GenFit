Neutral-Grip Pull-Ups

Description

Neutral-grip pull-ups target the lats, biceps, and upper back while placing less strain on the shoulders and wrists, making them a joint-friendly variation.

Posture Guide

- Grab the pull-up handles with your palms facing each other (neutral grip)
- Hang fully extended with your core tight
- Pull yourself up until your chin clears the bar
- Lower back down slowly and under control
- Avoid swinging or using momentum
