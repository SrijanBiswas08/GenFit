Wide-Grip Barbell Row

Description

The Wide-Grip Barbell Row is a compound back exercise emphasizing the upper lats, rear delts, and rhomboids.

Posture Guide

- Grab the barbell with a grip wider than shoulder-width
- Hinge forward at the hips keeping your back flat
- Pull the barbell toward your chest
- Squeeze the shoulder blades at the top
- Lower the barbell with control
