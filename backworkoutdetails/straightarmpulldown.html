Straight-Arm Pulldown

Description

The straight-arm pulldown isolates the latissimus dorsi and works as a great finishing movement for the back, improving mind-muscle connection and lat engagement.

Posture Guide

- Stand facing a cable machine with a straight bar attached to the high pulley
- Grasp the bar with an overhand grip, shoulder-width apart
- Keep your arms straight and pull the bar down to your thighs
- Control the bar as it returns to shoulder height without bending your elbows
- Engage your core and avoid arching your back
