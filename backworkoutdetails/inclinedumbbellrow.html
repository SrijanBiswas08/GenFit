Incline Dumbbell Row

Description

The incline dumbbell row isolates the upper and mid-back muscles by eliminating momentum, targeting the lats, rhomboids, and traps effectively.

Posture Guide

- Lie chest-down on an incline bench
- Hold dumbbells with arms extended
- Pull dumbbells towards your ribs
- Squeeze shoulder blades at the top
- Lower slowly to the starting position
