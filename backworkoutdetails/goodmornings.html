Good Mornings

Description

Good Mornings are a posterior chain exercise primarily targeting the hamstrings, glutes, and lower back. It involves hinging at the hips while maintaining a straight spine.

Posture Guide

- Place a barbell on your upper traps, similar to a back squat position
- Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
- Keep your chest up and core braced
- Hinge at the hips, pushing them back while keeping a neutral spine
- Lower your torso until it's nearly parallel to the floor
- Drive your hips forward to return to the starting position
