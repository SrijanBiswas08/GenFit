Back Extension

Description

The back extension is a bodyweight or weighted exercise that targets the lower back, glutes, and hamstrings by hinging at the hips while keeping the spine neutral.

Posture Guide

- Position yourself on a back extension bench with hips at the pad edge
- Cross arms over chest or hold a weight plate
- Lower torso slowly while maintaining a flat back
- Contract glutes and hamstrings to raise torso back to start position
- Avoid hyperextending the spine at the top
