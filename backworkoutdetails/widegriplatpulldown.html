Wide-Grip Lat Pulldown

Description

The wide-grip lat pulldown emphasizes the upper portion of the latissimus dorsi, helping develop width in the back and improve the V-taper look.

Posture Guide

- Sit at a lat pulldown machine and grab the bar with a wide overhand grip
- Keep your chest up and pull the bar down toward your upper chest
- Squeeze your lats at the bottom, then slowly return the bar to the top
- Avoid leaning too far back or using momentum
