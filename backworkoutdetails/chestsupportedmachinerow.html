Chest Supported Machine Row

Description

The chest supported machine row targets the upper back and lats while minimizing lower back strain, making it ideal for controlled muscle engagement.

Posture Guide

- Adjust the seat and chest pad to align with your torso
- Grasp the handles with a neutral grip
- Keep chest pressed against the pad
- Pull the handles back while squeezing shoulder blades
- Slowly return to the start position
