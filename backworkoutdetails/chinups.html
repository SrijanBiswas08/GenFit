Chin-Ups

Description

Chin-ups are a compound bodyweight exercise targeting the lats, biceps, and upper back, performed by pulling your body up until your chin clears the bar.

Posture Guide

- Grab the pull-up bar with an underhand grip, shoulder-width apart
- Hang with arms fully extended and feet off the ground
- Pull your body upward, keeping chest lifted and elbows driving down
- Pause at the top when your chin is above the bar
- Lower slowly with control back to the starting position
