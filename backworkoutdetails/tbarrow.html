T-Bar Row

Description

The T-Bar Row is a compound back exercise that primarily targets the middle back and lats, also working the traps and rhomboids.

Posture Guide

- Load one end of a barbell and secure the other end
- Straddle the bar with a close neutral grip handle
- Bend at the hips with a slight bend in the knees
- Pull the bar toward your chest while squeezing the shoulder blades
- Lower the bar under control and repeat
