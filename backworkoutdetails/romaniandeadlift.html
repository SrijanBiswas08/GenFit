Romanian Deadlift

Description

The Romanian Deadlift (RDL) emphasizes the hamstrings, glutes, and lower back, making it a great accessory for posterior chain development.

Posture Guide

- Start standing with the barbell at thigh level
- Hinge at the hips, push your hips back
- Keep a slight bend in the knees
- Lower the bar close to the legs until you feel a stretch in the hamstrings
- Keep your back neutral throughout
- Return to standing by driving hips forward
