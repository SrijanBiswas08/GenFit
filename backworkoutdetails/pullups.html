Pull-Ups

Description

Pull-ups are a compound bodyweight exercise that primarily works the lats, along with the biceps, traps, and other upper body muscles.

Posture Guide

- Hang from a pull-up bar with an overhand grip, slightly wider than shoulder-width
- Engage your core and pull your chest up toward the bar
- Keep your body straight and avoid swinging
- Lower yourself under control until arms are fully extended
