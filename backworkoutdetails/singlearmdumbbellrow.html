Single-Arm Dumbbell Row

Description

This exercise strengthens the upper back, lats, and biceps, enhancing posture and unilateral muscle balance.

Posture Guide

- Place one knee and hand on a bench for support
- Hold a dumbbell in the opposite hand
- Keep your back flat and core engaged
- Pull the dumbbell toward your hip
- Squeeze the back muscles at the top
- Lower with control
