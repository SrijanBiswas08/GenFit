Cable Pull-Through

Description

The cable pull-through is a hip hinge movement that strengthens the posterior chain, primarily targeting the glutes and hamstrings using a cable machine.

Posture Guide

- Set the rope attachment at the lowest pulley setting
- Face away from the machine and grip the rope between the legs
- Hinge at the hips, letting the rope pass behind you
- Drive through your heels and extend your hips to stand up
- Squeeze your glutes at the top and avoid hyperextending your back
