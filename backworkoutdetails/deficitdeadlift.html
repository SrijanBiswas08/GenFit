Deficit Deadlift

Description

The deficit deadlift is a deadlift variation performed while standing on a raised platform. It increases the range of motion and emphasizes strength off the floor, particularly targeting the hamstrings, glutes, and lower back.

Posture Guide

- Stand on a platform or weight plate (1-4 inches)
- Grip the barbell with your normal deadlift stance
- Keep chest up and back flat
- Drive through your heels and extend hips to stand tall
- Lower the bar under control without rounding your back
